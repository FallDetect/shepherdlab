Deployment

Up and running in a day.

No complex infrastructure. Works with standard IP cameras you may already own.

01
Camera installation

SafeWatch+ works with most standard IP cameras — including ones you may already own. Tapo, Hikvision, Reolink and others are supported. Camera performance may vary based on resolution, frame rate and placement. Our team advises on compatibility and optimal positioning before deployment.

02
Person enrolment

Each person walks naturally in front of the camera for 60 seconds. AI captures 20 samples automatically. Name them, set their monitoring level. Done.

03
AI self-calibration

SafeWatch+ learns each person's normal standing posture within minutes of first use — regardless of how high or at what angle the camera is mounted. By measuring each person's own baseline, fall thresholds are always relative to them, not the camera. A camera mounted 3m high and one mounted 1.5m high will both detect falls accurately for the same person. No manual configuration needed.

How it works: AI tracks hip position during normal standing activity → builds a personal baseline → fall threshold = significant deviation from that person's normal → works for any camera height, any room size, any person height.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is my camera footage stored in the cloud?

No. Raw video footage never leaves your device. All camera processing happens locally on the SafeWatch+ hardware in your home or facility. Only anonymised data — movement summaries and alert logs — is sent to the cloud for reporting.

Will SafeWatch+ work with cameras I already own?

SafeWatch+ is compatible with most standard IP cameras including Tapo, Hikvision and Reolink models. Camera performance may vary based on resolution, frame rate and positioning. We assess compatibility before every deployment and advise if any cameras need upgrading.

What happens if the internet goes down?

Core safety features — fall detection, local alerts and room monitoring — continue to work without internet. Cloud features such as weekly reports and AI fall probability scores will resume automatically when connectivity is restored. Your loved one is never left unprotected.

How does enrolment work? Does the patient need to cooperate?

No cooperation needed. The person simply walks naturally in front of the camera for 60 seconds during setup. SafeWatch+ captures 20 face samples automatically and builds their profile. For patients with limited mobility, even a few minutes of normal activity is sufficient.

How is SafeWatch+ installed and set up?

For home deployments, SafeWatch+ ships pre-configured and ready to use — plug in the device, connect your cameras, and our team completes the setup remotely via secure connection. Facility deployments include an on-site visit for camera placement, installation and staff training.

What if the hardware fails?

All subscription plans include remote diagnostics and support. If a hardware fault is confirmed, we ship a pre-configured replacement unit. During transit, we work with you to maintain monitoring using alternative methods. Facility plans include expedited replacement SLAs.

Can SafeWatch+ be used overseas?

Yes. The hardware ships internationally and once installed, is managed entirely remotely. Cloud features and Telegram alerts work globally. For facility deployments outside our home region, we work with certified local installation partners to handle on-site setup.
