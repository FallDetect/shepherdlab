Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

🔒 Core principle: Raw video footage from SafeWatch+ is never transmitted to any external server. All camera processing happens on your own hardware. Only anonymised data is used for cloud-based reporting.

Who we are

ShepherdLab is a division of NHB Industrial Pte Ltd (UEN 202542682D), 5038 Ang Mo Kio Industrial Park 2, #01-409, Singapore 569541. We build AI care monitoring technology for families and healthcare facilities.

What data we collect

Depending on the product you use, we may collect:

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment provider; we do not store card details
  • Device information — hardware identifiers for licence validation
  • Anonymised usage data — movement summaries, alert logs, health trend scores (no images, no video, no biometrics)
  • Support correspondence — emails and messages you send us

What data stays on your device

For SafeWatch+ users, the following data is processed and stored exclusively on the SafeWatch+ hardware in your home or facility and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Raw camera footage and video recordings
  • Face recognition embeddings and enrolled face data
  • Real-time skeleton tracking data
  • Local fall detection processing

How we use your data

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To generate health reports and AI fall risk scores (anonymised data only)
  • To send safety alerts via Telegram
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our AI models using aggregated, anonymised data

Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. We may share anonymised, aggregated data with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers for hosting and computing
  • Payment processors for subscription billing
  • No other third parties without your explicit consent

Data retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months. Anonymised analytics data may be retained longer for AI model improvement. You may request deletion of your account data at any time.

Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain uses of your data

Security

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. SafeWatch+ devices communicate with our servers via secure encrypted connections. Access to customer data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

Contact us

For privacy enquiries or to exercise your rights, contact us at hello@shepherdlab.life or through our contact page.
