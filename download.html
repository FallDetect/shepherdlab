Common questions
Where do I find my Shopee order number?
Open the Shopee app → tap Me → My Purchases → find your FallGuard+ order → tap it → the Order ID is shown at the top of the order details page.
I didn't receive the email — what do I do?
Check your spam or junk folder first. The email comes from hello@shepherdlab.life. If it's not there after 5 minutes, email us at hello@shepherdlab.life with your Shopee order number and we'll send credentials manually within 24 hours.
What phone do I need?
Any Android phone from 2019 or newer with a front camera and at least 3GB RAM. Android 9.0 or above. Check: Settings → About phone → Android version. iPhone is not supported in this version.
How do I install an APK file?
Download the APK from the link in your email. Tap the downloaded file. Android will ask you to allow installation from unknown sources — tap Settings and enable it. Then tap Install. The full guide is included in your email.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes. Email hello@shepherdlab.life and we'll upgrade or downgrade your account. You only pay the difference on your next billing cycle.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your order number and email are stored securely. All monitoring data is processed on your own phone — nothing is uploaded to external servers without your consent. We are PDPA compliant under NHB Industrial Pte Ltd (UEN 202542682D).
