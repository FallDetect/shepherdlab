What FallGuard+ Does

More than fall detection.

A complete monitoring solution — catching problems before they become emergencies.

⚠️
Fall Detection

Side and forward falls detected in under 3 seconds. Alarm on phone + Telegram alert to caregiver.

🪑
Posture Deterioration

Gradual slumping detected before it becomes a fall. Preventive alert gives caregivers time to act.

🚶
Absence Detection

Alert fires when patient is absent from wheelchair for 30+ seconds — may have left unassisted.

😤
Agitation Alert

Sustained unusual movement detected. Indicates discomfort, pain or distress before it escalates.

📳
Inactivity Alert

Configurable reminder after prolonged stillness. Helps prevent pressure sores with timely repositioning.

😴
Head Drop Detection

Drowsiness or potential medical episode detected. Head drops below threshold triggers immediate alert.

🎙️
Distress Sound

Microphone monitors for cries, coughing or distress sounds. Alert sent with audio context.

📈
Daily Fall Risk Score

AI computes Low / Medium / High risk score daily based on posture trends and activity patterns.

📅
Weekly PDF Report

Auto-emailed to family every Sunday. Falls, posture trends, AI recommendations. Pro plan.

FAQ

Common questions.

What phone do I need? +
Any Android or iPhone from 2019 onwards works. iOS supported. Most families use an old spare phone.
Does the patient need to do anything? +
Nothing at all. The phone mounts on the wheelchair and monitors automatically. No wearables, no buttons to press.
How does Telegram work? +
Install Telegram (free), message @userinfobot to get your Chat ID, enter it in FallGuard+. Takes 2 minutes. All alerts come to your Telegram.
Does it work outdoors and in transport? +
Yes. FallGuard+ works anywhere the phone has a view of the patient and an internet connection — parks, hospital trips, taxis.
Where is my data stored? +
All data is processed and stored on-premise — on your own hardware, in your own facility. Nothing leaves your site. We do not share or sell your data.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from within the app or by emailing us.
